This tool will let you check the "shiny value" of a player or of an egg (a value between 0 and 8191 for Gen III to V, and between 0 and 4095 for Gen VI and up). If the egg and the player's SVs are the same, the resulting Pokémon will be hatched shiny. This tool can also calculate Pokémon Shiny Value (PSV), just use the ESV section.

Note: This tool is only accurate for Pokémon games Gen III and up. Don't input decimal PID—convert them to hexadecimal first.

If the calculated TSV becomes pink, that means the inputed IDs can be used for the Cute Charm Glitch in Gen IV (DPPtHGSS). For a full list click here.
